What or Who is The Mothman?

Information about The Mothman

The Mothman is a creature from West Virginia folklore, this creature was first reported to be seen in an area called Point Pleasant from November 12th, 1966 to December 15th, 1967. The Mothman is commonly described to have red eyes, claws for feet and a human-like black body with a 7 foot wingspan.
